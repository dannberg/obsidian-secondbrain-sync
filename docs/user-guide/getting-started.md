# Getting Started

Welcome to Second Brain Digest! This guide will walk you through setting up your account and receiving your first digest.

## Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Visit [secondbraindigest.com](https://secondbraindigest.com) and click **Sign Up**
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your inbox for a magic link - click it to complete signup
4. You're now logged in to your dashboard

## Step 2: Install the Obsidian Plugin

To generate digests, Second Brain Digest needs access to your vault data. This is done securely through our Obsidian plugin.

1. Open Obsidian and go to **Settings > Community plugins**
2. Disable **Restricted mode** if prompted
3. Click **Browse** and search for "Second Brain Digest"
4. Install and enable the plugin
5. See [Connecting Your Vault](connecting-vault.md) for detailed setup instructions

## Step 3: Configure Your Settings

Once your vault is synced, customize your digest experience:

- **Timezone** - Ensure digests arrive at the right time for you
- **Modules** - Choose which sections to include in your digest
- **Schedule** - Set when you want to receive your daily email

See [Settings & Preferences](settings.md) for a full walkthrough.

## Step 4: Generate Your First Digest

You can generate a digest immediately from your dashboard:

1. Go to your **Dashboard**
2. Click **Generate Digest**
3. Check your email for your first personalized digest
