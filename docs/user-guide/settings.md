# Settings & Preferences

Customize your Second Brain Digest experience through the settings page. This guide covers all available options.

## Accessing Settings

1. Log in to your account
2. Click **Settings** in the navigation menu

## General Settings

### Email Address

Your digest emails are sent to this address. To change it:

1. Enter your new email address
2. Click **Save**
3. Verify the new address via the confirmation email

### Timezone

Setting your timezone ensures:

- Digests arrive at the scheduled time in your local time
- "This Day in History" shows the correct date
- Activity timestamps are accurate

Select your timezone from the dropdown and save.

## Module Configuration

### Enabling/Disabling Modules

Each digest module can be turned on or off:

1. Find the **Modules** section
2. Toggle individual modules on/off
3. Disabled modules won't appear in your digest

### Module Order

Customize the order modules appear in your digest:

1. Drag and drop modules to reorder them
2. The preview updates to show the new order
3. Save your changes

See [Modules Overview](../modules/overview.md) for details on each module.

## AI Settings

### AI Provider

Choose which AI provider generates your insights:

- **OpenAI** - Uses GPT models for analysis
- **Rule-based** - Uses pattern matching (no AI, faster)

If no AI provider is configured, rule-based analysis is used automatically.

### AI Features

When AI is enabled, you get:

- Summarized daily recaps
- Intelligent connection suggestions
- Context-aware complementary insights

## Sync Settings

### Exclusion Rules

Manage what gets synced from your vault:

- **Excluded Folders** - Folders that won't be synced
- **Excluded Tags** - Notes with these tags are skipped

Changes here sync to your Obsidian plugin automatically.

### Sync Status

View your current sync status:

- Total notes synced
- Last sync time
- Any sync errors

### Clear Vault Data

To remove all synced data:

1. Click **Clear Vault Data**
2. Confirm the action
3. Your notes will be removed from our servers
4. You'll need to re-sync to generate digests

## Account Settings

### API Tokens

Manage tokens for the Obsidian plugin:

- View active tokens
- Generate new tokens
- Revoke existing tokens

### Delete Account

To permanently delete your account:

1. Go to **Account > Delete Account**
2. Confirm by typing your email
3. All data will be permanently removed
